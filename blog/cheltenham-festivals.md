# A Year of Culture: A Guide to the Cheltenham Festivals

**Published on:** December 8, 2025 | **Author:** Manus AI | **Category:** Events

![Cheltenham Jazz Festival](/images/events/cheltenham-jazz.jpg)

Cheltenham is known as the “Festival Town” for good reason. It hosts a year-round program of world-class festivals, covering everything from jazz and literature to science and music. These festivals attract some of the biggest names in their respective fields, and they offer a fantastic opportunity to experience the best of culture and entertainment. Here’s a guide to the four main Cheltenham Festivals.

## Cheltenham Jazz Festival (April/May)

Kicking off the festival season in style, the Cheltenham Jazz Festival is one of the UK’s leading jazz festivals. The festival features a diverse program of music, from established stars to emerging talent, and it covers a wide range of genres, from traditional jazz to contemporary and experimental music. The festival has a lively and friendly atmosphere, and many of the events are free.

## Cheltenham Science Festival (June)

The Cheltenham Science Festival is a six-day celebration of science and technology. The festival features a wide range of talks, workshops, and interactive events, and it’s a great way to learn about the latest scientific discoveries and to meet some of the world’s leading scientists. The festival is a fun and engaging event for all ages, and it’s a great way to get your kids excited about science.

## Cheltenham Music Festival (July)

The Cheltenham Music Festival is one of the oldest and most respected music festivals in the country. The festival features a wide range of classical music, from large-scale orchestral concerts to intimate chamber music recitals. The festival also has a strong focus on new music, and it commissions a number of new works each year. The festival is a must-visit for any classical music lover.

## The Times and The Sunday Times Cheltenham Literature Festival (October)

The Cheltenham Literature Festival is the oldest and one of the most prestigious literature festivals in the world. The festival features a star-studded lineup of authors, poets, and thinkers, and it covers a wide range of topics, from fiction and poetry to history and politics. The festival is a great place to discover new writers, to hear your favorite authors speak, and to get your books signed.
