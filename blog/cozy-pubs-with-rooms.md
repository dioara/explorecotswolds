# Ales and Pillows: 5 of the Coziest Pubs with Rooms in the Cotswolds

**Published on:** December 8, 2025 | **Author:** Manus AI | **Category:** Stay

![The Swan Inn](/images/restaurants/swan-inn.jpg)

For a truly authentic Cotswolds experience, there's nothing quite like staying in a traditional pub with rooms. These historic inns offer a unique blend of cozy charm, hearty food, and local character, and they're the perfect base for exploring the region's beautiful countryside. Here are five of the coziest pubs with rooms in the Cotswolds.

## 1. The King's Head Inn, Bledington

This quintessential Cotswold pub has been named "Pub of the Year" by The Good Pub Guide on multiple occasions, and it's easy to see why. The pub has a beautiful village green setting, a cozy and traditional interior, and a reputation for excellent food. The bedrooms are simple but comfortable, and the pub is a great base for exploring the surrounding countryside.

## 2. The Lamb Inn, Burford

This historic inn is located on a quiet side street in the beautiful town of Burford. The pub has a cozy and traditional feel, with open fires, exposed beams, and a beautiful walled garden. The bedrooms are all individually designed, and many have antique furniture and original features. The pub is a great place to relax and unwind after a day of exploring the town.

## 3. The Swan Inn, Swinbrook

This charming riverside pub was a favorite of the famous Mitford sisters, and it's easy to see why. The pub has a beautiful setting on the banks of the River Windrush, and it has a relaxed and informal atmosphere. The bedrooms are all individually designed, and many have views of the river. The pub is a great place to enjoy a pint of local ale and a plate of fish and chips.

## 4. The Wheatsheaf Inn, Northleach

This 17th-century coaching inn has been given a stylish and contemporary makeover, but it still retains its historic charm. The pub has a popular restaurant, a cozy bar, and a collection of beautifully designed bedrooms. The hotel also has a beautiful garden, which is the perfect place to relax with a G&T on a summer's evening.

## 5. The Five Alls, Filkins

This beautiful 18th-century pub is located in the charming village of Filkins. The pub has a cozy and traditional interior, with open fires, flagstone floors, and a beautiful garden. The bedrooms are all individually designed, and many have antique furniture and original features. The pub is a great place to enjoy a delicious meal and a pint of local ale.
