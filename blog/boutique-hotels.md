# Cotswolds Charm with a Contemporary Twist: 5 of the Best Boutique Hotels

**Published on:** December 8, 2025 | **Author:** Manus AI | **Category:** Stay

![Cotswold House Hotel](/images/hotels/cotswold-house.jpg)

If you love the idea of a Cotswolds getaway but prefer a more contemporary and intimate setting than a grand manor house, then a boutique hotel could be the perfect choice for you. These smaller, design-led hotels offer a unique and personal experience, with stylish interiors, excellent service, and a focus on local character. Here are five of the best boutique hotels in the Cotswolds.

## 1. Cotswold House Hotel & Spa, Chipping Campden

Located in the heart of the beautiful town of Chipping Campden, Cotswold House Hotel & Spa is a stylish and sophisticated boutique hotel with a relaxed and welcoming atmosphere. The hotel has a beautiful spa, a two-rosette restaurant, and a collection of individually designed bedrooms, some of which have their own private gardens and hot tubs.

## 2. The Painswick, Painswick

This 18th-century rectory has been transformed into a chic and stylish boutique hotel with a playful and contemporary edge. The hotel has a beautiful restaurant with panoramic views of the Painswick Valley, a cozy bar with an open fire, and a collection of beautifully designed bedrooms. The hotel also has a spa treatment room and a games room.

## 3. No. 38 The Park, Cheltenham

Located in a beautiful Georgian townhouse in the fashionable Montpellier district of Cheltenham, No. 38 The Park is a stylish and elegant boutique hotel with a home-from-home feel. The hotel has a beautiful lounge with an honesty bar, a private dining room, and a collection of individually designed bedrooms with beautiful antique furniture and contemporary art.

## 4. The Old Stocks Inn, Stow-on-the-Wold

This 17th-century coaching inn has been given a stylish and contemporary makeover, with bold colors, quirky design features, and a fun and friendly atmosphere. The hotel has a popular restaurant, a cozy bar, and a collection of individually designed bedrooms. The hotel also has a secret garden, which is the perfect place to relax with a G&T on a summer's evening.

## 5. Wild Thyme & Honey, Ampney Crucis

This beautifully restored 16th-century pub and hotel is the epitome of rustic chic. The hotel has a collection of beautifully designed bedrooms, some of which have their own private terraces and outdoor bathtubs. The hotel's restaurant serves delicious modern British cuisine, and the cozy bar is the perfect place to relax with a pint of local ale.
