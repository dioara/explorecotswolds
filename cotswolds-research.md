# Cotswolds Research Data

## Top Attractions & Villages

### Most Famous Villages
1. **Bibury** - Famous for Arlington Row, considered one of the most beautiful villages
2. **Castle Combe** - Picture-perfect main street, very photogenic
3. **Bourton-on-the-Water** - "Venice of the Cotswolds", has Cotswold Motoring Museum, Birdland Park & Gardens
4. **Lower Slaughter** - Picturesque limestone cottages along River Eye
5. **Upper Slaughter** - Beautiful village on River Eye
6. **Burford** - Historic market town
7. **Cirencester** - "Capital of the Cotswolds", historic Roman town
8. **Stow-on-the-Wold** - Market town
